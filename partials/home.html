Laura Sandoval

Software Engineer / Scientist

Laura is a Software Engineer at the Laboratory for Atmospheric and Space Physics (LASP) supporting the IMAP Science Operations Center. She designs and operates real-time space weather systems that ingest and process telemetry from global ground stations for NASA missions, with a focus on I-ALiRT, scientific data pipelines, and cloud-native architecture on AWS.

Projects

Laura's work spans real-time telemetry ingest and distribution via I-ALiRT, AWS CDK infrastructure and science data workflows for IMAP, and SPICE-based geometry and coordinate transform pipelines for heliophysics missions.
